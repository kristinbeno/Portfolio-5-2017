About

I designed my first website at the age of 9.

Was it hosted on Angelfire? Yes.
Did I use a drag-and-drop website builder to build it? Yes.
Was it Pokemon themed? That’s irrelevant.

The point is, I’ve been passionate about all things web-related from a very young age, and there are few things in life that get me more excited than a well-designed digital experience. Since my Pikachu days, I’ve been lucky enough to have tried my hand in a variety of mediums—print design, online banner animation, basic website design—before eventually grabbing on to software design. From upfront discovery and research to rapid iteration and talking through interactions with the dev team, I love every aspect of my job. Because in the end, there’s no greater satisfaction than creating an interface that people understand AND enjoy using.
