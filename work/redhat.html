Red Hat Enterprise Linux Microsite

← Previous Project Next Project →

Details

Background

Red Hat is the leading provider of open-source software products for the enterprise community. They came to us looking for a microsite targeted primarily to IT architects looking to extend their data centers. The purpose of this site was to accommodate an abundance of content, from video to white papers to generate interest around Red Hat Enterprise Linux.

Role

I was the lead visual designer for this project. This website concept featured complex background animations and interactions, therefore I worked closely with the front-end developer in the early stages of the design process, ensuring that it would be functional and usable on all devices.
