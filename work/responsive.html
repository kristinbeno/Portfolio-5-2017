Buildium Responsive Website Template

← Previous Project Next Project →

Overview

Problem
Buildium’s free website templates were outdated and not optimized to be viewed on mobile devices. As a result, our customers were missing out on a lot of potential tenants.
Solution
Provide Buildium customers with a responsive website that their tenants and prospects can use on any device.
Results
We’re currently collecting data for this project. Stay tuned for an update!

Details

Background

One of Buildium’s differentiating offerings is a professional website template included with all subscriptions. This allows customers to pull unit vacancies from their Buildium account and post them directly onto a public-facing website.

The website template was built over 7 years ago and didn’t have responsive capabilities. Additionally, we offered customers over 10 different template options, all with different color schemes.

Knowing that the market has changed, and many tenants are searching for apartments on their mobile phones, we wanted to update the website to be mobile-friendly. We also wanted to preserve the ability to choose a customized colored palette that works with all our customer’s brands.

Role

Designed and built a working prototype of the responsive template. Coordinated with product managers to ensure that requirements were met. Worked alongside engineers in build process to map color scheme colors to other Buildium templates.
