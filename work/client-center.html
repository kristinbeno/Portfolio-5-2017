All Property Management Client Center

← Previous Project Next Project →

Overview

Problem
Customers were having difficulty completing key tasks in the Client Center. As a result, APM account managers were spending too much of their time helping customers navigate the interface and making account changes on the customers' behalf.
Solution
Improve the overall usability of the Client Center to reduce friction and enable customers to edit account details in a self-service manner. Not to mention refresh the design to establish a strong sense of identity for the APM brand.
Results
After the redesign, we saw an 18% increase in the number of customers that gave a rating of 5 or higher (out of 7) when asked the SEQ (Single Ease Question).

Details

Background

All Property Management (APM) is a lead-generation service for property management companies looking to grow their portfolios. For a variable price per lead, APM connects clients with property owners seeking management services. Customers are given access to an online portal, where they can edit their lead pricing, coverage area, marketing messaging and deposit funds into their account.


Role

As the lead UX designer on this project, I redesigned the application from the ground up, making improvments to the site’s information architecture, user interface, and descriptive copy. I also worked with our UX researcher to develop usability and first-click test plans, as well as identifying UX metrics by which we’d later measure the project’s success.
