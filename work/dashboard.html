Buildium Dashboard

← Previous Project Next Project →

Overview

Problem
Many Buildium customers weren’t finding the presentation of data on the homepage particularly useful, other than using it as a shortcut to get to important pages in the application.
Solution
Implement a homepage dashboard that surfaces important customer data in a consumable way, enabling the customer to spend their time in the application more efficiently.
Results
The MVP of the homepage dashboard was released to trial accounts for 5 weeks. During this time, we saw a 40% average increase in the number of trialers converting in the first week.

Details

Background

Buildium is a SaaS product for residential and association managers. Their all-in-one solution supports all facets of the property management business, from accounting to maintenance tracking to tenant/owner communication.

Ever since its inception back in 2006, Buildium has featured the same homepage design. However, feedback from our customers, as well as user engagement data, indicated that it was under-utilized. Knowing the amount of data our customers store in their accounts, we decided to better utilize the homepage by bringing more compelling information to the forefront.

Role

As lead designer on the project, I was responsible for the card content, UI design and interactions. I also assisted with customer interviews and worked collaboratively with front and back-end developers.
